Privacy Policy

PureCinema is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Information We Do NOT Collect

PureCinema does not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Any data sent to third-party analytics services

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

To enhance your experience, PureCinema stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Search History: Your recent search queries are saved locally to help you quickly access previous searches. This data never leaves your device.
  • Favorites: Movies and TV shows you mark as favorites are stored locally on your device.
  • App Preferences: Your chosen theme, font size, and sort preferences are saved locally.

All locally stored data can be cleared at any time through the app's settings or by deleting the app.

Third-Party Services

PureCinema uses the following third-party service:

  • The Movie Database (TMDB) API: We use TMDB to fetch movie and TV show information, including titles, descriptions, release dates, cast information, and poster images. When you search for content, your search query is sent to TMDB's servers to retrieve results. Please refer to TMDB's Privacy Policy for information on how they handle data.

Data Security

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal data at risk. All app preferences and favorites are stored locally on your device using Apple's standard secure storage mechanisms.

Children's Privacy

PureCinema does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age. The app is designed to be safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Your Consent

By using PureCinema, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
